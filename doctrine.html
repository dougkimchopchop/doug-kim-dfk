Doctrine
The pattern
Planning is the primary avoidance behavior. Building systems without moving into execution. Every conversation seeking frameworks is itself an instance of the pattern.
Identity follows action
The DFK identity is not a feeling to access — it is a behavior to perform until the feeling catches up. Votes are cast through actions, not intentions.
The two-minute start
Not finishing, not being good — just opening the thing and staying for two minutes. The only protocol for breaking avoidance conditioning.
Financial stress
Unresolved money pressures create persistent background threat load that compounds avoidance. Back taxes first. This is not metaphorical.
Food and consumption
When creative output isn't generating reward, dopamine-seeking through eating or screens fills the gap. The fix is increased output, not willpower tactics.
The teacher structure
Best work happened under external forcing function, strict deadlines, contained scope. Recreating those conditions — not adding tactics — is the actual lever.
